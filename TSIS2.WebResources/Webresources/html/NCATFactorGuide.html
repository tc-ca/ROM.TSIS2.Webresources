The actions of the regulated party were intentional(it was not an accident). The regulated party committed the non-compliance(s) even if they did not intend the consequence or harm.

  • Does the regulated party, at the company or site level, have any history of similar or identical non-compliance(s)?

Criteria for Low Criteria for Medium Criteria for High
There is no history of non-compliance(s)? There is some history of non-compliance(s) but not similar. There is either a history of similar non-compliance(s) or a general pattern of non-compliance(s) which shows a disregard for following the rules.

  • Did the non-compliance(s) benefit the regulated party by saving money, time or other resources?
  • Was/were the non-compliance(s) done with a view to obtaining a benefit or competitive advantage? Or to disadvantage a competitor?

Criteria for Low Criteria for Medium Criteria for High
There was no direct or indirect economic benefit obtained. There was some actual benefit or advantage obtained but indirectly. There was actual economic benefit obtained and the non-compliance(s) enabled the realization of that benefit.

  • If there was harm, did the regulated party take responsibility and mitigate the harm or did the regulated party try to avoid responsibility?
  • When there was no harm, did the regulated party take steps to address the potential recurrence of the non-compliance(s)?

Criteria for Low Criteria for Medium Criteria for High
The regulated party accepted responsibility and mitigated the harm. The regulated party accepted some responsibility and some attempts at mitigation were undertaken. The regulated party did not accept responsibility for the non-compliance(s) and/or did not attempt to mitigate its effects.

  • Was the regulated party cooperative during the inspection and responsive to the requests of the TSI?

Criteria for Low Criteria for Medium Criteria for High
The regulated party assisted and facilitated throughout. The regulated party assisted when asked but was not actively engaged in facilitating the inspection. The regulated party assisted only when asked and actively or passively obstructed or attempted to obstruct the work of the Inspector.

Criteria for Low Criteria for Medium Criteria for High
The non-compliance(s) was/were reported by the regulated party (reporting could take place during a routine inspection). The non-compliance(s) was/were discovered during a routine inspection before any harm might have occurred. The non-compliance(s) was/were only discovered as a result of an incident and would have continued but for the discovery during a reactive inspection.
